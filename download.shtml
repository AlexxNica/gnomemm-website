Source Packages

The source packages for gtkmm are available on the sourceforge download area.

Requirements

  • GTK+
  • libsigc++ (1.0.x for gtkmm 1.2 and 1.2.x for gtkmm 2)

GTK+

You can download GTK+ and GNOME from download.gnome.org.
