  • April 2004: gtkmm and glibmm 2.4.0 , as per the GNOME schedule. gtkmm 2.4.0 wraps new API in GTK+ 2.4 and installs in parallel with gtkmm 2.2.
  • October 2003: gtkmm 2.3.0 was released. This will become the new stable, parallel-installable, gtkmm 2.4 in March 2004.
